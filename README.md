📁 Dashboard Sections

1. Revenue by Campaign
Bar chart comparing revenue across 5 campaign types — Influencer (~5.76bn), Social Media (~5.75bn), Paid Ads (~5.75bn), SEO (~5.99bn), and Email (~5.60bn) — all performing at a similar scale.

2. ROI by Campaign
Horizontal bar chart showing ROI per campaign. Social Media leads at ~31K, followed by Paid Ads, Influencer, SEO, and Email (all ~30K).

3. Revenue by Channel Used
Treemap highlighting top revenue-generating channels — Instagram (16.39M), WhatsApp (16.18M), Facebook, YouTube (15.97M), and Email (15.87M).

4. Target Audience by Channel Used
Horizontal bar chart showing reach per channel across audience types. Instagram, YouTube, and WhatsApp top the chart at ~3.2K each; Google and WhatsApp at the lower end (~0.7K).

5. Duration by Channel Used
Donut chart showing time share across channels — Instagram (969,215), WhatsApp (54,646), and YouTube (54,488) as top contributors.

6. Channel Used by Customer Segment
Donut chart breaking down channel usage by segment — College Students (20.33%), Working Women (19.96%), Youth (19.90%), Premium Shoppers, and Tier 2 City all with near-equal distribution.

7. Leads by Target Audience
Line/area chart showing leads volume — Premium Shoppers lead at ~21.0M, followed by College Students (~21.35M), Working Women (~20.68M), Tier 2 City (~20.38M), and Youth (~20.35M).
